# BUG-005 — Inconsistent Product Model Naming

## Summary
Several products contain inconsistent or unclear model naming values in the Products list.

---

## Environment
- OpenCart Local Environment
- XAMPP
- Admin Panel

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Login to Admin Panel
2. Navigate to Catalog → Products
3. Observe Product Name and Model columns

---

## Actual Result
Some products contain unclear or generic model values.

Examples:
- MacBook → Product 16
- MacBook Air → Product 17
- Nikon D300 → Product 4

---

## Expected Result
Product models should follow clear and meaningful naming conventions.

---

## Severity
Medium

---

## Priority
Medium

---

## Notes
Issue impacts usability and product management clarity but does not block functionality.
