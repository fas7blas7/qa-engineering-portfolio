# BUG-003 — Inconsistent Product Model Naming

## Summary
Several categories don't have consistent representive image in the Categories list.

---

## Environment

- OpenCart Local Environment
- XAMPP
- Admin Panel

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Login to Admin Panel
2. Navigate to Catalog → Categories
3. Observe Image column

---

## Actual Result

Most categories dont have representive picture.

Examples:
Cameras
Components
Components > Mice and Trackballs

---

## Expected Result

Category pictures should be present.

---

## Severity

low

---

## Priority

Medium

---

## Impact

Issue impacts usability and product management clarity but does not block functionality.
